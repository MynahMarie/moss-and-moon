Dear friend, thank you for landing here. This is a safe place to nurture vulnerable communication so that we can grow more connected to one another. You have the option to keep your thought private (only I will read it) or to allow me to share it publicly, either on the Moss & Moon website, on social media, in my newsletter, or in a future zine. Please keep in mind that saying yes isn't a guarantee that I will share it.

If you do choose to allow me to share your thought (thank you!), please also write down under which name you'd like your thought to appear. Please note that I am collecting your email in the form below, **but I will not use it unless you explicitly ask to be added to my newsletter**. Also, **I will never share it with anyone**. I respect your privacy.

Thank you for your openness and vulnerability, and for your support in this project. Remember that you are amazing, beautiful, and strong.

Much love,
<br>
Mynah from Moss & Moon
